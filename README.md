## Description

Keep Moving Forward is a game that aims to build empathy for people living with depression. Players control Rachel as she navigates a maze to collect three keys and progress to the next level. However, Rachel’s energy randomly depletes, slowing her movement and darkening her vision to represent the fatigue commonly experienced by those with depression. Throughout the maze, players can find hearts that restore Rachel’s energy, symbolizing the importance of support and care from others.

---

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

- Open Github link to launch the game
- Use arrow keys or WASD keys to move
- Press 'R' to reset or 'H' to open the help screen
- Make your way through the maze and collect all three keys to open the door
- Collect hearts to restore energy

## Iteration Notes

a. Post-Playtest:

- Change #1: We changed the environment to be a maze instead of an open field to make the game more challenging
- Change #2: We had hearts respawn 10 seconds after use so players could use them continously throughout the game
- Change #3: We added a "Help" Screen to make the game easier for first-time players to understand

b. Post-Showcase

- Planned improvement #1: We plan on making the game more challenging by creating a more complex maze layout and either adding a time limit or introducing obstacles at certain points in the map
- Planned improvement #2: We plan on adding audio and improving visuals through sprite animation and changing the colour of the energy bar to improve accessibility
